A Community of Grace

Discover Our Purpose

Welcome to Pemuda Advent (PA) at Universitas Klabat. We are a youth ministry dedicated to fostering spiritual growth, cultivating meaningful relationships, and extending God's love to our campus and beyond.

Our mission is to nurture a generation of young people who are deeply rooted in faith, committed to service, and united in Christ. Whether through worship, fellowship, or community outreach, we seek to embody the light of hope in everything we do.

Vision

To be a radiant community of youth, reflecting Christ's love and preparing for His soon return.

Mission

To reach up to God, reach in to our fellow youth, and reach out to the world through active service.
